About STU-Bot

STU-Bot is an AI-powered chatbot created to assist students, staff, and visitors of Sunyani Technical University by providing instant and accurate answers to frequently asked questions.Whether you're looking for information on admissions, courses, schedules, or campus life, STU-Bot is designed to be your helpful companion 24/7.

Developed using advanced natural language processing technologies and trained on carefully curated data from the university, STU-Bot simplifies access to information while reducing the workload on administrative staff. Its intuitive interface makes it easy for users to engage and receive support in real time.

Our mission with STU-Bot is to enhance communication, improve service delivery, and create a more accessible digital experience for all members of the STU community. As technology evolves, STU-Bot will continuously learn and improve to better serve its users.
